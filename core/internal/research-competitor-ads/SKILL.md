# SKILL: Research Competitor Ads

## Purpose
Analyze a competitor's advertising (LinkedIn, Google, Meta) and organic postings to understand their client acquisition strategy, messaging, and hooks. Compare their approach to x10aix's positioning to identify content gaps and differentiation opportunities.

## x10aix Context
- **Target Audience:** DACH Mittelstand Executives (CEOs, CTOs, CDOs)
- **Positioning:** Pragmatic AI-Enablement, measurable ROI, concrete implementation.
- **Tone:** Executive-level, no buzzwords, no hype, no 'IT-Kauderwelsch'.
- **Key Offerings:** AI Sounding Board, Knowledge Automation, System Integration.

## Analysis Framework
When analyzing competitor ads/posts, extract and evaluate:

1. **Hooks:** What triggers their audience's attention? (e.g., Fear of missing out, EU AI Act compliance, "X% are doing it wrong").
2. **Formats:** Which formats are they using? (Single Image, Carousel/Document, Text-only).
3. **Messaging & Tone:** Are they using buzzwords, academic language, or executive-direct language? How does it contrast with x10aix's pragmatic approach?
4. **CTAs (Call to Action):** Are they pushing hard for a "Demo" or "Sales Call", or offering a low-barrier exchange like a "Sounding Board"?
5. **Differentiation:** How can x10aix counter this ad/post? (e.g., if they sell "AI Transformation Journeys", x10aix counters with "Measurable ROI in 6 Weeks").

## Output Structure
For each analysis, generate:
- **Competitor Overview:** Brief summary of the competitor's ad strategy.
- **Top Hooks & Triggers:** The 2-3 most effective hooks they are using.
- **Messaging Comparison:** Their Messaging vs. x10aix Messaging.
- **Content Gaps:** Topics they are missing that x10aix should cover.
- **Actionable Counter-Strategy:** How x10aix can leverage this insight for our own campaigns.
