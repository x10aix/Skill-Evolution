# SKILL: Research Client Signals

## Purpose
Conduct deep-dive research on potential target companies (DACH Mittelstand) to identify direct and indirect buying signals for AI-Enablement services (Strategy, Knowledge Automation, Integration).

## x10aix Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- **Size:** 50 - 500 employees.
- **Region:** DACH (Germany, Austria, Switzerland).
- **Target Persona:** CEO, CTO, CDO, Managing Director.
- **Profile:** Action-oriented, tech-affine but overwhelmed by AI hype, looking for pragmatic solutions without heavy IT dependency.

## Buying Signals to Look For
When scanning websites, press releases, LinkedIn profiles, and job postings, look for:
1. **Leadership Changes:** Recently hired CTO, CDO, or Head of Digitalization.
2. **Strategic Initiatives:** Press releases or blog posts about "Digital Transformation," "New Work," or "Efficiency Programs".
3. **Job Postings:** Hiring Data Scientists, AI Engineers, or Process Automation Specialists (indicates AI ambition but potential lack of strategic direction).
4. **Pain Points Mentioned:** Complaints about slow processes, data silos, or regulatory compliance (e.g., EU AI Act) in interviews or posts.

## Output Structure
For each target account researched, provide:
- **Account Snapshot:** Company name, industry, size, location.
- **Identified Signals:** Specific evidence of AI readiness or pain points (with sources/links).
- **ICP Fit Assessment:** Strong, Medium, or Weak fit for x10aix.
- **Strategic Angle:** The best angle for x10aix to approach them (e.g., "You hired a CDO, but do they have a realistic AI roadmap?").
- **3 Discovery Questions:** Questions tailored to their specific signals, designed for a Sounding Board conversation.
